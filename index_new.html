About the Ministry

The Federal Ministry of Women Affairs is committed to promoting the welfare and advancement of women, children, and other vulnerable groups in Nigeria. Our mission is to create an enabling environment for the full integration of women into national development.

Our Mandate

The broad mandate of the Ministry is to advise the government on Gender and Children's issues, including matters affecting Persons with Disabilities and the Aged; initiate policy guidelines and lead the process of gender equality and mainstreaming at both the National and International levels.

Our Vision

To help build a Nigerian Society that guarantees equal access to social, economic and wealth creation opportunities to all, irrespective of gender, places premium on protection of the child, the aged and persons with disabilities.

Our Mission

To promote the development of women, children, and other vulnerable groups through policy formulation, advocacy, and implementation of programs that enhance their welfare and socio-economic empowerment.

Who We Are

Federal Ministry of Women Affairs and Social Development was created consequent upon the response to The United Nations agreement to establish Institutional Mechanisms for the Advancement of Women. The Ministry is charged with the responsibility of promoting the welfare of women, children, and other vulnerable groups in Nigeria.

We work to ensure that the rights of women and children are protected and that they have equal access to education, healthcare, and economic opportunities.
